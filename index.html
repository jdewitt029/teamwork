Teamwork

Animation in AfterEffects | 1920x1920 | 0:07 | 2021

This project is a short seven second loop that I animated using AfterEffects. I created a story using simple shapes and colors to create a clean and cohesive design. Anime was an inspiration for this project and I wanted to incorporate some of the animation techniques they use. Despite these shapes being primitive circles, they actually perform complex maneuvers and actions. The story starts with the two circles trying to get through the middle barrier and bouncing right off of it. They decide to check if they can run around the wall but that is of no success either. Finally, they decide to crash into the wall at the same spot using all their force to try to get through. They end up merging together for a second then they separate and the loop restarts.
